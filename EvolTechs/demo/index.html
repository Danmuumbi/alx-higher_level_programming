Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our collaboration process.

What types of organizations do you collaborate with?

We collaborate with a diverse range of organizations including startups, established companies, academic institutions, non-profits, and government agencies. What matters most is alignment with our project goals and potential for mutual benefit.

How long does the evaluation process typically take?

For demo requests, we typically respond within 2 business days to schedule a session. For partnership proposals, our evaluation process takes 3-5 business days for initial review, followed by additional time for technical assessments if needed.

What are the criteria for selecting partners?

We evaluate based on several factors: technical compatibility, potential impact on the project, alignment with our mission, scalability of the solution, and the partner's capacity to contribute meaningfully to the collaboration.

Are there any costs associated with collaboration?

There are no costs to request a demo or submit a proposal. For approved partnerships, financial arrangements vary depending on the nature of the collaboration and are discussed transparently during the partnership formation process.

How are intellectual property rights handled?

Intellectual property rights are clearly defined in our partnership agreements. Typically, pre-existing IP remains with the original owner, while new developments are shared according to the terms of the specific collaboration agreement.
