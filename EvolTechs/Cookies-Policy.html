Cookies Policy

Learn how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Last Updated: This Cookies Policy was last updated on June 15, 2023 and applies from that date.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., authentication, security) Session or Persistent
Performance Cookies Help us understand how visitors interact with our website Persistent (1-2 years)
Functionality Cookies Remember your preferences and settings Persistent (up to 1 year)
Targeting/Advertising Cookies Used to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance Persistent (up to 1 year)

4. Managing Cookies

4.1 Browser Settings

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish through your browser settings. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

For more information about how to manage cookies in specific browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
