What is your typical software development process?

We follow an agile, iterative approach that emphasizes collaboration and continuous improvement:

  1. Discovery Phase: Requirements gathering, feasibility analysis, and planning
  2. Design Sprint: Rapid prototyping and UX/UI design validation
  3. Development Iterations: 2-week sprints with regular deliverables
  4. Quality Assurance: Continuous testing and refinement
  5. Deployment: Staged rollout with monitoring
  6. Maintenance & Support: Ongoing optimization and updates

This methodology ensures we deliver maximum value quickly while remaining adaptable to changing needs.

What is the interview process like at EvolTechs?

Our hiring process is designed to be thorough yet efficient:

  1. Initial Screening: Resume review and recruiter phone screen (30 min)
  2. Technical Assessment: Coding challenge or design exercise (1-2 hours)
  3. Technical Interviews: 2-3 sessions with team members (45-60 min each)
  4. Cultural Fit Interview: Discussion with leadership about values and work approach (1 hour)
  5. Offer Extended: Successful candidates receive an offer within 3-5 business days

We aim to complete the process within 2-3 weeks while respecting candidates' time throughout.
